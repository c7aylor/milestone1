about me

Hi. I’m Carrie, web designer and developer based in central Texas. The web has become my playground since I have turned a lifelong love of art & language and a curiosity about tech into skills I use to help others have their own space on the web. I’ve spent many years as a mom at home, and recently I’ve decided to get back to work doing something that will allow me to use my creativity and eye for detail while providing flexibility for time with my family. That’s what landed me in tech and I'm so thankful for this opportunity.
